The target audience for the Skagit Washington Chamber of Commerce website is twofold. First the businesses of the area and those who might be wanting to set up a business within the city. Second are those who might want to research the local businesses primarily the local residents of the city and also travelers planning to visit.

Persona 1

  • Occupation:Current business owner.
  • Demographics and Education:59 years old with an MBA
  • Goals for using the site:Learn how the chamber can help with finding other businesses that can complement their own in cooperative arrangements.
  • Social:Very outgoing. Always looking for anther contact or business associate
  • Technology:Very savy but not highly skilled. Always looking for technology to improve business workflow. Needs support of professional IT staff.
  • Quote:If you are not moving forward you are moving back.

Persona 2

  • Occupation:Housewife
  • Demographics and Education:BA in childhood education
  • Goals for using the site:Find the right businesses to approach for charity work.
  • Social:Very outgoing. Instrumental in organizing the community.
  • Technology:Laymen's level of understanding. Basic office suite proficiency. Needs help with higher level tasks.
  • Quote:I love giving back to the community that I grew up in.

Scenarios

  1. Where can I find specific types of businesses in my city?
  2. What are the priorities of the customers here?
  3. Who is the most involved in the charity work of the community?
  4. How do I get a business license?
  5. What is the tax structure for retail shops?
